How It Works

1. Discover & Save

Scan for nearby Bluetooth devices and save them with custom names for easy identification.

2. Launch Radar Search

When you lose a device, select it from your saved list and start the radar search mode.

3. Follow the Signal

Move around while watching the radar and listening to audio beeps. As you get closer to your device, the beeps speed up and the signal strength increases.

4. Find Your Device

When the signal is strongest, you've found your device! The app will show you're right on top of it.
